About Me

Hello! I'm Michael Bonnet. I am a Flight Software Engineer at Terran Orbital, student at the University of Alabama (MS Aerospace Engineering, 2025), and alumnus of the University of Texas at Arlington (Computer Science, May 2022).



I'm an enthusiast in software applications for space and aerospace, especially in the realm of autonomy. I fell in love with my code making real-life things move and haven't looked back. My skills and interests lie primarily in the fields of AI/machine learning, robotics, aerospace, and autonomous vehicles. My strong work ethic, interpersonal skills, industry experience, and technical prowess make me a great team member, student, and learner.



When not working or studying, I enjoy learning about aviation, doing personal AI/ML/robotics projects, watching and playing basketball, gaming, exercising, and volunteering.
